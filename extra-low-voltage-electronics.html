Extra Low Voltage-Electronics

Headed by a COREN registered Electronic Engineer of several years of experience, our ELV-Electronics Division is adequately equipped with both hard and soft capabilities for all aspects of electronic systems.

We continue to collaborate with electronics equipment providers so as to benefit from their equipment-specific exposure and trainings.

All relevant manpower in this division continue to be trainned and retrained so as to be able to consistently out-perform the competition.

We have acquired hands-on installation experience spanning the following aspects of electronics technologies:

  • Access Control
  • Building Management System, BMS
  • Computer network
  • Telecommunication & Communication
  • Fire detection and alarm system
  • Television distribution
  • Close circuit distribution
  • Uninterruptible Power supply systems
  • Green power equipment, etc.

We pride ourselves for consistently being both very competitive and for timely delivery.
