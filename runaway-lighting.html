Runaway Lighting

TAEWOO airfield lighting business is based on the highly reputable airfield lighting equipment and technical supports of ATG Airports UK Ltd.

TAEWOO LP LTD's airfield lighting division offers ATG Airports UK Ltd's airfield lighting products which are world-wide appreciated for their quality for both civil, military airports, helipads and heliports.

These airfield lighting products are offered by ATG Airports UK Ltd in both the incandescent and the IRIS LED versions.

ATG Airpprts UK Ltd's range of airfield lighting products for runways:

  • Runway Lighting
  • Taxiway Lighting
  • Approach / Threshold Lighting
  • Low Intensity Lighting
  • Taxiway Guidance Signs
  • Heliport Lighting

ATG Airports UK Ltd's airfield lighting power solutions.

  • Micro 100 Constant Current Regulator
  • Micro 200 Constant Current Regulator
