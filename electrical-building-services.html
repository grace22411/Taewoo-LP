Electrical Building Services

Headed by a COREN registered Electrical Engineer of several years of experience, our Electrical Building Services Division is adequately equipped with both hard and soft capabilities for electrical building services provision in every type of building; domestic, commercial and industrial.

Very often in Electrical building services, the installation scope includes the provision of Medium Voltage power systems, the Medium Voltage specialists of our MV power Distribution Division would step in to consistently deliver efficient, safe and reliable power system.

Most modern buildings require a variety of Extra Low Voltage (or Electronics) devices to function properly, our ELV-Electronics Division specialists would step in to provide highly professional services in Access Control, Building Management Systems, Fire Alarm etc.
